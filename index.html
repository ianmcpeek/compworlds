Bruno!

About the Game

Experience the premier side-scrolling, action packed, simulator of school life at UW Tacoma. Step into the shoes of Bruno, a Computer Science student trying to earn a degree with nothing but his smarts and a backpack full of sandwiches. Face off against the dangerous faculty and collect food items to stay energized in the fight for an education.

Currently the game has the first level completed, but more levels are coming soon!


Controls:

move left & right

jump with space bar

throw sandwich with X
